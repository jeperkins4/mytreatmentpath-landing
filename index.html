Frequently Asked Questions

Is MyTreatmentPath free?

Yes, completely free and open source. Download, use, and even modify the code. No hidden costs, no subscriptions.

Is my health data secure?

Absolutely. Data is stored locally on your device with SQLCipher encryption. If you enable cloud sync, you control the Supabase instance and encryption keys. We never see your data.

Do I need an internet connection?

For basic tracking, no. Research search, AI summaries, and cloud sync require internet, but core features work offline.

Can I import my medical records?

Currently supports Foundation One CDx genomic reports. More import formats (labs, EHR exports) coming soon. You can also manually enter any data.

Does this replace my doctor?

No. MyTreatmentPath is a research and organization tool, not medical advice. Always consult your healthcare providers for treatment decisions.

Windows/Linux version?

Coming soon! macOS is the initial release. Windows and Linux builds are in progress. Follow on GitHub for updates.
